How does it work?

One of the things that makes flashcards memorisable is the work you put into the making of the cards.

Creating your own flashcards helps you understand then content in it and remember it.

Find a video you would like to learn from and start here. We check the video in the background if it's compatible. It might happen that the video is not compatible.
Mark the interesting parts of the video. For example from 00:00:20 to 00:00:45.
Collect all the files. You can download extracted parts and start creating your own cards.
